Our Subjects

Subjects We Offer

We focus on STEM subjects.

Mathematics

Algebra : Equations, expressions, and functions Geometry : Shapes, measurements, and spatial reasoning Trigonometry : Triangles, angles, and real-world applications Statistics & Probability : Data analysis, averages, graphs, and predictions Intro to Calculus : Rates of change and basic integrals (at higher grades) Mathematical Modeling : Solving real-life problems using math Computational Math : Estimation, coding, and numerical methods Logic & Problem Solving : Patterns, reasoning, and critical thinking

Science

Physics : Motion, forces, energy, electricity, waves, and simple machines Chemistry : Atoms, elements, chemical reactions, acids & bases, and matter Biology : Cells, genetics, human body systems, ecosystems, and evolution Earth & Environmental Science : Climate, natural resources, geology, and sustainability Scientific Method : Observations, hypothesis testing, experiments, and data analysis Technology Integration : Using tools, simulations, and digital labs in investigations STEM Applications : Real-life experiments, innovations, and cross-disciplinary projects

Technology

Digital Literacy : Understanding computers, internet safety, and digital tools Information & Communication Technology (ICT) : Software, hardware, and data handling Coding & Programming : Basics of coding languages (like Python, HTML, JavaScript) Robotics & Automation : Building and programming robots, sensors, and control systems Computer Systems : How computers work: hardware, software, storage, and networks Cybersecurity : Online safety, data privacy, and responsible tech use Design & Innovation : Using tech tools to create apps, websites, models, and solutions Emerging Technologies : AI, VR/AR, 3D printing, IoT, and smart systems

Arts

Visual Arts : Drawing, painting, sculpture, printmaking, photography, and design Art Elements & Principles : Line, shape, color, texture, space, balance, contrast, and rhythm Art History & Theory : Study of local and global art movements, artists, and cultural influences Media & Digital Arts : Graphic design, animation, video editing, and digital illustration Crafts & Applied Arts : Textile work, ceramics, beadwork, and mixed media Creative Process : Brainstorming, planning, creating, reflecting, and refining artwork Art Critique & Analysis : Describing, interpreting, and evaluating art pieces Careers in the Arts : Introduction to creative industries like design, fashion, animation, and fine arts
